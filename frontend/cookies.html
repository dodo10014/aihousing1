Cookies Policy

Last updated: 2025

This Cookies Policy explains how AiHousing (“we”, “us”, “our”) uses cookies and similar tracking technologies when you visit our website.

1. What are cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device that help websites operate efficiently, remember preferences, enhance performance, and provide analytics.

2. How we use cookies

AiHousing uses cookies to:

  • Enable secure login and account sessions
  • Store your cookie preferences
  • Improve website performance
  • Analyse traffic and usage patterns
  • Support payment processing (Stripe)

3. Types of cookies we use

A) Essential Cookies (Always Active)

These cookies are required for the site to function:

  • Session authentication
  • CSRF protection
  • Load balancing & security
  • Cookie preference storage

These cannot be disabled.

B) Performance & Analytics Cookies

Used to understand how visitors interact with the site.

  • Page views & navigation patterns
  • Error tracking
  • Website optimisation

C) Functionality Cookies

  • Remembering form inputs
  • Saving your previous reports (local cache)
  • Improving user experience

D) Third-party Cookies

These may include:

  • Stripe (for payments)
  • Analytics platforms (privacy-friendly, anonymised)

AiHousing does NOT use:

  • Advertising cookies
  • Cross-site tracking
  • User profiling or remarketing cookies

4. Managing cookie preferences

You can manage your cookie preferences at any time using the AiHousing Cookie Banner.

You may also disable cookies through your browser settings. However, disabling essential cookies may cause parts of the site to stop working.

5. How long cookies remain

Cookies may be:

  • Session cookies (deleted when you close the browser)
  • Persistent cookies (remain up to 12 months)
