Privacy Policy
This Privacy Policy explains how AiHousing collects, uses and protects your information when you use our website and AI-powered property report services.
1. Who we are
AiHousing provides AI-assisted property insights for UK buyers, renters and investors. When we say “we”, “us” or “AiHousing” in this policy, we are referring to the AiHousing service and website located at aihousing.uk.
2. What information we collect
We may collect:
  • Contact details such as your email address when you contact us or create an account.
  • Property details such as postcodes, addresses or listing links you enter.
  • Photos you upload for analysis by our AI models.
  • Technical data such as IP address, device type and browser information.
  • Usage data such as pages visited, features used and the time spent on our site.
3. How we use your information
We use your information to:
  • Generate property reports based on your inputs and uploaded photos.
  • Improve the accuracy and performance of our AI models.
  • Operate, maintain and secure our website and services.
  • Respond to your questions and provide support.
  • Comply with legal obligations and prevent fraud or abuse.
4. Photos and property data
  • Your photos are processed to generate a report and may be stored for a limited period for quality checks and to improve our models.
  • We do not publish your photos or property reports publicly.
  • We may aggregate and anonymise data (for example, statistics across many properties) for analytics and product improvement.
5. Legal basis for processing (UK & EU GDPR)
We process your personal data based on:
  • Contract: to provide the property report service you request.
  • Legitimate interests: to improve our service, prevent abuse and understand how our platform is used.
  • Consent: where required, for analytics cookies or marketing communications.
6. Data sharing
We may share data with:
  • Service providers such as hosting, analytics or payment partners (for example, Stripe).
  • Professional advisers (legal, accounting) where necessary.
  • Authorities, where required by law or to protect our rights or users.
We do not sell your personal data.
7. International transfers
Some of our service providers may be located outside the UK/EEA. Where this happens, we take steps to ensure your data is protected, for example by using standard contractual clauses or ensuring the provider offers adequate protection.
8. Data retention
We keep your data only for as long as necessary:
  • Account and report data – while your account is active and for a reasonable period afterwards.
  • Photos – typically for a limited period after report generation, unless you ask us to delete them sooner.
  • Invoices and transaction records – as required by tax and accounting laws.
9. Your rights
Under data protection laws, you may have the right to:
  • Access the personal data we hold about you.
  • Request correction of inaccurate data.
  • Request deletion of your data, where applicable.
  • Object to certain types of processing.
  • Ask us to restrict processing in some circumstances.
  • Request a copy of your data in a portable format.
You can exercise these rights by contacting us using the details below.
10. Cookies and analytics
We use cookies and similar technologies to:
  • Keep the site secure and functioning properly.
  • Understand how the site is used (analytics).
  • Remember your choices or logged-in status.
You can control cookies via your browser settings and our cookie banner. For more details, please see our Cookie Policy.
11. Payments
Payments for AiHousing reports are processed by third-party provider Stripe. We do not store your full card details on our own servers. Stripe processes your payment data as an independent controller in accordance with its own privacy policy.
12. Contact
If you have any questions about this policy or how we handle your data, you can contact us at:

Email: support@aihousing.uk
13. Changes to this policy
We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our service or the law. The latest version will always be available on this page, with an updated “last updated” date.
